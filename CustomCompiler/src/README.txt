SUPPORTED : 
-----------------------------------------------
1. Expressions :
- simple number;
- word;
- hex number;
- unary number;
- string;
- constants (PI , GOLDEN_RATIO , E);
- conditional (== , != , >= , <= , && , etc.);
- binary for numbers (= , + , * , /);
- binary for strings (+ , *).
-----------------------------------------------
2. Statements :
- assignment (x = 2 , y = x + 5);
- if , else (if (...) ... , else ...);
- print (print text).
-----------------------------------------------
